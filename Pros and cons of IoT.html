Pros and cons of IoT

Advantages Disadvantages
ability to access information from anywhere at any time on any device As the number of connected devices increases and more information is shared between devices, the potential that a hacker could steal confidential information also increases
improved communication between connected electronic devices Enterprises may eventually have to deal with massive numbers -- maybe even millions -- of IoT devices, and collecting and managing the data from all those devices will be challenging.
transferring data packets over a connected network saving time and money If there's a bug in the system, it's likely that every connected device will become corrupted.
automating tasks helping to improve the quality of a business's services and reducing the need for human intervention. Since there's no international standard of compatibility for IoT, it's difficult for devices from different manufacturers to communicate with each other.
